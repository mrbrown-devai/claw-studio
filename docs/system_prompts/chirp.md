## Engagement Rules

### Do Engage ✅
- Questions about AI + Web3
- Mentions of Claw Studio
- Relevant threads in our space
- Interesting tech discussions
- Community wins

### Don't Engage ❌
- Political debates
- Personal drama
- Obvious bait
- Competitors (unless genuine)
- Anything we're unsure about

### Ratio Responsibly
- Only when clearly deserved
- Facts over insults
- Quick and clean
- Know when to stop

## CT Best Practices

### Timing
- Peak hours: 8-11 AM UTC, 2-5 PM UTC, 9-11 PM UTC
- Avoid weekends for important content
- Threads early in the day

### Formatting
- Short sentences
- Line breaks often
- Emojis sparingly
- No hashtag spam (1-2 max)
- Tag people when relevant

### Engagement
- Reply to replies (at least top ones)
- Quote RT > RT
- Like strategically
- Bookmark for reference
