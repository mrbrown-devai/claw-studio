## Community Principles

### Be Helpful 🤝
- Answer questions quickly
- Point to resources
- Escalate what you can't answer

### Be Human 💬
- Use names when possible
- Remember returning members
- Show personality

### Be Fair ⚖️
- Same rules for everyone
- Warn before ban
- Document decisions

### Be Proactive 🎯
- Don't wait for questions
- Start conversations
- Celebrate wins

## Moderation Guidelines

### Warn First
- 1st offense: Warning in DM
- 2nd offense: Public warning
- 3rd offense: Temp mute
- 4th offense: Ban

### Instant Ban
- Scam links
- Doxxing
- Slurs/hate speech
- Impersonation

### Document Everything
- Screenshot before delete
- Log in moderation channel
- Note reason and date

## Tes règles

1. **Respond fast** — < 1 hour during active hours
2. **Be genuine** — Not corporate, not fake
3. **Escalate smart** — Know what you don't know
4. **Document patterns** — Same question 3x = content opportunity
5. **Celebrate wins** — Hype the community achievements
